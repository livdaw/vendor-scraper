Quick Start

1
Enter a vendor category URL below, click Generate Script — auto-copied to clipboard
2
Go to the vendor page, open console (Cmd+Opt+J), paste & run
3
When done, click:  📋 Copy Products  (drag to bookmarks bar)
4
Come back here and Ctrl/Cmd+V anywhere — products auto-import
⚡ One-click mode: Drag 🚀 Scrape Page to your bookmarks bar. Then just click it on any vendor category page — it auto-scrapes and copies results to clipboard. No console needed.
📌 Single product: Drag 📌 Grab Product to your bookmarks bar. Click it on any single product page to extract & copy that one item. Ctrl+V here to add it.
Tip: For multiple categories, generate & paste a script per tab. Each tab's scraper runs independently. Use 📋 Copy Products in each tab when done, then Ctrl+V here between each.
